About the Campaign

The Danger

Drug abuse destroys not just individuals, but rips apart the fabric of families and societies. It steals potential and clouds the future.

🌅

Our Vision

A vibrant, drug-free Punjab where every youth is empowered by education, health, and opportunity, leading to a prosperous tomorrow.

🤝

Our Resolve

We stand united—government, citizens, and communities—to eradicate this menace through awareness, compassion, and strict action.
